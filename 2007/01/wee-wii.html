Jan 16, 2007 - Wee Wii

Lots of people wondered what Nintendo were thinking when they called it the Wii. It all sounded a little close to wee. Surely nobody would buy it. But who knew that the choice of name would lead to a death? Well, it happened… and all because of a Hold Your Wee for a Wii competition. It never would have happened if they’d just called it something innocuous like the revolution.
